About me.

Killtheheretik is positioned to set the standard for a new sound, he has an energetic atmosphere that intertwines with an inspiring melody, and pulls you out from your seat into a new world. Killtheretik has been very musical from an early age, from hardcore bands to freestyle rap, he’s always found a way to stimulate vocal creativity.

In 2017 Killtheheretik was able to truly identify his sound and run with it. Since then Killtheretik has gone on to release several EPs as well as the album “DIGITAL DANGER” in 2020. “DIGITAL DANGER” was a defining moment for Killtheheretik and the next chapter looks very promising. This is one of those artists you’ll wish you would’ve been listening to from the start!
